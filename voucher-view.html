Umrah Travel Instructions

  • Arrive at airport 4 hours before departure
  • Keep passport, visa, tickets & valuables in hand carry
  • Save emergency contacts on phone & keep a written copy handy
  • Always keep hotel address/card with you in Makkah & Madinah
  • Follow group leader instructions at all times during Umrah & Ziyarat (in case of group travel)
  • Do not leave luggage unattended at any time
  • Check baggage allowance before packing
  • Be at boarding gate 4560 minutes before departure
  • Keep luggage tag & booking copy safe during travel
  • Hotel check-in: 16:00-18:00 hrs | Check-out: 12:00 hrs (Saudi Hotels)
