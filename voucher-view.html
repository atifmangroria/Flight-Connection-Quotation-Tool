✅ Umrah Travel Checklist

Please ensure you have the following before departure:

  • Valid passport (at least 6 months validity, 2 blank pages)
  • Umrah visa (check expiry date and conditions)
  • CNIC copy (1 original + 2 photocopies)
  • Passport-sized photographs (white background, at least 6 copies)
  • Flight tickets (printed copy + digital copy)
  • Hotel confirmation letters
  • Travel insurance documents
  • All required vaccinations (consult your doctor)
  • Sufficient local currency (Saudi Riyals)
  • International debit/credit card
  • Ihram clothing (2 sets for men)
  • Comfortable prayer clothes
  • Prayer mat, Quran, tasbih
  • Personal medication with doctor's prescription
  • Mobile phone and universal charger/adapter
  • Emergency contact numbers saved on phone
  • Photocopy of all documents (stored separately)
  • This voucher (printed or saved offline)
